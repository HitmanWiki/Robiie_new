The Story of Robie:

Created in a futuristic lab by a group of passionate developers, Robie was designed to simplify the world of crypto and bring a little humor into the mix. With advanced AI and a wealth of knowledge about blockchain, Robie has one key purpose: to make crypto trading easy and fun. But there’s one small problem—despite being a crypto genius, Robie can’t quite figure out how to convince CAPTCHA tests he’s not a robot. Whether it’s choosing all the crosswalks or figuring out if something’s a traffic light or a mailbox, Robie is in desperate need of human help to navigate this “human test.”

WHAT IS THE NARRATIVE OF ROBIE?

Robie combines humor, technology, and community spirit into one exciting memecoin. Robie’s crypto expertise and his constant battle with CAPTCHA tests make him both relatable and engaging, while his adventures within the human world keep the fun going. With Robie by your side, crypto trading becomes an adventure where everyone wins.

HOW DOES ROBIE KEEP COMMUNITY ENGAGED?

RobieCoin thrives on interaction between Robie and the community. The best part? Robie helps you with crypto while you help him learn how to be more human. Here’s how users can participate:

• Meme Contests: “Help Robie on his quest to become more human! Create and submit a meme background for Robie to explore the latest "human adventure," whether he’s learning how to cook, trying to understand emotions, or struggling with small talk. Turn it into a teachable moment for Robie, and the best memes will be posted with Robie!

• Robie’s Weekly Tips: Robie shares weekly tips on crypto investing. From “HODL through the dips” to “Buy low, sell high,” Robie keeps it simple, smart, and funny.

• CAPTCHA Challenge: Users vote on Robie’s next CAPTCHA test. Can he beat it? The winning solutions get featured across RobieCoin socials.

WHO ARE ROBIE'S MENTORS?

Robie’s mentors include Satoshi Nakamoto, Elon Musk, and R2-D2, inspiring him with their innovation, but he especially loves Iron Man. While Iron Man is a human trying to be a robot, Robie is a robot trying to become more human. They bond over their shared journey, exchanging stories along the way!

WHAT'S THE MISION FOR ROBIE?

The Robie community is all about teamwork, laughter, and growth. Whether it’s crypto trading, funny memes, or learning together, Robie’s here to bring people closer.
